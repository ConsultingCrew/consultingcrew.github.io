HR Analytics Dashboard — Workforce Visibility

An Excel dashboard that transformed fragmented HR reporting into a clear, real‑time overview for an HR consultancy.

Business Challenge

An HR consultancy relied on fragmented spreadsheets to analyze workforce performance, attrition, and employee trends. Reporting was time‑consuming and lacked a centralized view.

Consulting Crew Approach

Consulting Crew developed an interactive Excel dashboard to simplify workforce reporting. The dashboard included:

  • Headcount tracking
  • Attrition analysis
  • Department‑level insights
  • Monthly trend reporting
  • KPI cards for workforce metrics

Business Impact

  • Reduced reporting effort
  • Improved access to workforce trends
  • Enabled faster review of HR metrics
  • Supported more informed workforce discussions
“The dashboard made workforce reporting easier to review and present.”

Project Details

Client: RNack Management (HR Consultancy)

Tools: Microsoft Excel (Power Query, Pivot Charts, Slicers)

252 Employees Tracked
9.5% Attrition Rate

Services Provided
