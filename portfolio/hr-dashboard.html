HR Analytics Dashboard

Workforce visibility & reporting for an HR consultancy managing 500+ employees across multiple departments.

Business Challenge

An HR consultancy managing workforce data for multiple client organizations faced challenges with fragmented data, manual reporting, and lack of real‑time visibility into workforce trends.

Consulting Crew Approach

We developed a comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard in Excel that consolidates data from multiple sources into a single, unified view.

  • Data consolidation from multiple sources
  • Automated reporting with dynamic charts
  • Real‑time headcount and attrition tracking
  • Department‑level drill‑down capability
  • Interactive filters for flexible analysis
"We were spending 80% of our time on data preparation and only 20% on actual analysis. This dashboard flipped that ratio completely."

— HR Director, HR Consultancy Firm

Key Dashboard Features

  • Headcount Overview: Total employees, new hires, and departures with month‑over‑month trends
  • Attrition Analysis: Voluntary vs. involuntary turnover with department breakdown
  • Demographic Insights: Age, gender, and tenure distribution across the organization
  • Department Performance: Key metrics by department with color‑coded indicators
  • Hiring Funnel: Time‑to‑hire and source effectiveness metrics
  • Interactive Filters: Drill down by department, location, or date range

Business Impact

  • 80% Time Savings: Dramatic reduction in report preparation time
  • 95% Data Accuracy: Significant improvement in data consistency
  • Real‑Time Decisions: Immediate visibility enabling faster strategic decisions
  • Enhanced Client Satisfaction: Faster, more accurate reports delivered to clients
