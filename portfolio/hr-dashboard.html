HR Command Centre — Workforce Analytics

Excel‑based workforce command centre for an HR consultancy, delivering instant strategic clarity across attrition, diversity, and headcount trends.

Business Challenge

RNack Management provides HR consultancy to mid‑sized companies. To advise clients effectively, they needed a consolidated, real‑time view of workforce data spanning attrition, diversity, headcount trends, and salary distribution. Previously, they relied on static spreadsheets that took days to compile and offered no visual trending. By the time reports were ready, the problems they highlighted had often already escalated.

Consulting Crew Approach

We designed an interactive Excel dashboard with:

  • Slicers for department, gender, education level, and job grade — instant filtering for any client segment.
  • KPI cards for total headcount, gender ratio, average tenure, attrition rate, YoY growth, and absenteeism.
  • Dynamic charts including a monthly headcount trend, attrition by department bar chart, and a salary band analysis by grade.
  • A clean, anonymised data model that pulls from HRIS exports and refreshes in minutes.

The dashboard was built entirely in Microsoft Excel using Power Query, Pivot Charts, and slicers — no expensive BI tools required.

Business Impact

  • Faster recommendations: Attrition hotspots (e.g., IT at 13.8%) now spotted immediately, enabling targeted retention before quarterly reviews.
  • Stronger client presentations: Dashboard became the centrepiece of quarterly HR strategy sessions, replacing dense tables.
  • Reporting time reduction: From over 2 days to under 1 hour per client — consultants freed for high‑value advisory.
  • New business win: RNack secured a retainer by showcasing the dashboard’s real‑time capabilities in a proposal.
“The dashboard gave us an immediate, data‑backed view of where our client needed to act. It’s now the backbone of our HR strategy sessions.” — Director, RNack Management
