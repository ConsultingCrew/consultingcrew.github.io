Dadex — Modernizing a 65‑Year Legacy

Redesigned marketing collaterals, annual reports, and social media graphics that refreshed a manufacturing leader's corporate image.

Business Challenge

Dadex has been a trusted industry leader for over 65 years. However, many of their marketing materials had remained unchanged for years and no longer reflected the professionalism and innovation of the organization.

Consulting Crew Approach

We worked closely with the marketing team to redesign key communication materials, transforming technical content into visually engaging assets:

  • Redesign of corporate brochures and technical marketing materials
  • Development of visually structured annual reports
  • Creation of social media graphics for national events and awareness campaigns
  • Establishment of a consistent visual communication style

Business Impact

  • Modernized Corporate Image: Updated marketing materials aligned with contemporary design standards
  • Improved Audience Engagement: Social media engagement increased significantly within the first 60 days
  • Clearer Technical Communication: Complex product information presented in accessible, visually appealing formats
"The team helped us turn technical data into beautiful designs that our customers and stakeholders truly appreciate."

— Marketing Manager, Dadex
