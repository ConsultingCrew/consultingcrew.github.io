5‑Year Sales Trend Analysis

Multi‑division, multi‑region Excel dashboard revealing hidden product declines and regional growth pockets for strategic planning.

Business Challenge

BuildMac (Piping, Roofing, Plastics divisions) had five years of sales data scattered across dozens of files. Strategic planning relied on gut feel because no one could see the full picture of regional performance or product trends.

Consulting Crew Approach

We consolidated all historical data into a single Excel dashboard with slicers for fiscal year, division, product, and region. Trend charts revealed FC‑Piping revenue declining from 1,105 to 700 million PKR while PVC‑U volume rose. Regional breakdowns identified Central as the fastest‑growing region.

Business Impact

  • Strategic shift: Resources redirected toward PVC‑U and Central region based on clear evidence.
  • Board reporting time cut by 40+ hours.
  • Justified a dedicated regional sales manager, contributing to a 15% uptick the following year.
“The 5‑year dashboard finally gave us a clear picture of where we are winning and where we need to dig deeper. It’s now part of every board pack.” — CFO, BuildMac
