Five‑Year Sales Trend Dashboard

Bringing together multiple years of sales data to reveal long‑term patterns and guide strategic planning.

Business Challenge

Historical sales data existed across multiple files and reporting sources, limiting visibility into long‑term trends. Management needed a consolidated view for planning and analysis.

Consulting Crew Approach

Consulting Crew combined multiple years of sales data into a unified dashboard environment.

  • Trend analysis by year
  • Product category performance
  • Regional comparison
  • Sales office contribution analysis
  • Filtered business insights

Business Impact

  • Improved long‑term reporting visibility
  • Reduced time spent gathering historical data
  • Supported structured business review discussions
  • Enabled easier access to trend information
“The dashboard helped bring multiple years of information into one place.”
