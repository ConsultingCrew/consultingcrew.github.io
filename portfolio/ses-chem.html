SES-Chem: The Trading Athlete

Chemical Trading – Regional

Business Challenge

SES-Chem’s philosophy, "The Trading Athlete," represents agility, speed, and competitive performance. However, their existing brand identity did not reflect this dynamic positioning.

Consulting Crew Approach

We translated the company’s philosophy into a bold and energetic visual identity system. The new branding incorporated vibrant colors and modern typography designed to communicate movement, confidence, and competitiveness.

  • Development of a dynamic new logo reflecting the "Trading Athlete" concept
  • Creation of a cohesive corporate stationery system
  • Application of the brand identity across all official communication materials

Business Impact

  • Distinctive Brand Identity: A bold and memorable visual identity within the chemical trading sector
  • Professional Corporate Communication: Consistent branding across all official documentation
  • Enhanced Executive Confidence: Strengthened professional image in international business meetings
"Our new branding perfectly captures our energy. We now feel much more confident when meeting with international partners." — Director, SES-Chem
