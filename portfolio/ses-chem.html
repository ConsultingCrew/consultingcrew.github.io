SES‑Chem — The Trading Athlete

A bold visual identity that translates speed, agility, and competitive spirit into a memorable chemical trading brand.

Business Challenge

SES‑Chem’s philosophy of “The Trading Athlete” promised speed and competitiveness, but their outdated identity looked static and forgettable — a mismatch that weakened credibility with international partners.

Consulting Crew Approach

We translated the philosophy into a dynamic visual system: a forward‑leaning logo, high‑contrast electric‑blue‑and‑black palette, and angular typography suggesting motion. A cohesive stationery set extends the energy across every touchpoint.

Business Impact

  • Distinctive, memorable brand inside a conservative trading sector.
  • Consistent professional communication that commands confidence in global meetings.
  • Executive team reported feeling “ready to walk into any boardroom.”
“Our new branding perfectly captures our energy. We now feel much more confident when meeting international partners.” — Director, SES‑Chem
