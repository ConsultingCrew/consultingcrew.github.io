SES‑Chem — Translating Philosophy into Visual Identity

A bold visual system that reflects the company’s “Trading Athlete” philosophy and enhances professional confidence.

Business Challenge

SES‑Chem wanted a stronger visual identity that reflected its energetic positioning and business philosophy. The existing branding lacked distinction and did not effectively communicate confidence or professionalism.

Consulting Crew Approach

Consulting Crew designed a refreshed branding system aligned with the company’s positioning. The new identity focused on clarity, confidence, and consistency across communication materials.

  • Logo design
  • Corporate identity system
  • Branded stationery
  • Visual styling framework

Business Impact

  • Improved consistency in business communication
  • Created a more distinctive visual identity
  • Strengthened confidence during meetings and presentations
  • Improved overall brand presentation
“The branding better represents how we want to present ourselves professionally.”
