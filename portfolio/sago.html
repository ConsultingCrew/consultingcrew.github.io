SAGO — eCommerce That Performs

Custom online store with a mobile‑first checkout that transformed a traditional retailer into a digital competitor.

Business Challenge

SAGO’s retail operation was entirely offline. To survive and grow, they needed an e‑commerce platform that could handle online browsing, payments, and order management — and be so smooth that customers wouldn’t bounce.

Consulting Crew Approach

We developed a custom e‑commerce website focused on speed and clarity. Clear product categorisation, high‑quality visuals, and a three‑step checkout. The entire experience was built mobile‑first, because that’s where their customers are.

Business Impact

  • Successful online launch with a platform ready for scalable growth.
  • Streamlined shopping journey that customers find genuinely easy.
  • Seamless experience across smartphones and tablets.
