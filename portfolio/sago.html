SAGO — Transitioning from Traditional Retail to Online Selling

A custom e‑commerce platform that enabled a traditional retailer to enter the online market with a smooth shopping experience.

Business Challenge

SAGO wanted to expand beyond physical retail operations and enter the online market. The business required a complete e‑commerce platform capable of presenting products clearly while making online shopping simple for customers. The key challenge was to build a platform that balanced functionality with ease of use.

Consulting Crew Approach

Consulting Crew designed and developed an e‑commerce website focused on usability, navigation clarity, and responsive performance. The project emphasized customer experience and a streamlined shopping journey.

  • Product categorization for easier browsing
  • Mobile‑responsive shopping experience
  • Clear checkout flow
  • Product‑focused layouts
  • Organized product presentation

Business Impact

  • Enabled SAGO to establish an online retail presence
  • Improved accessibility for customers across devices
  • Simplified the product browsing and checkout experience
  • Created a scalable platform for future product growth
“The website gave us a strong foundation for online selling and improved how customers interact with our products.”
