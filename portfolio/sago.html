SAGO — Digital Retail Transformation

Custom e‑commerce platform that brought a traditional retailer online with a smooth mobile‑first shopping experience.

Business Challenge

A traditional retailer needed to establish an online presence to reach customers beyond their physical store. The business required a mobile‑optimized e‑commerce platform that would provide a seamless shopping experience while maintaining brand consistency.

Consulting Crew Approach

We designed and developed a custom e‑commerce platform tailored to the retailer's specific product categories and customer base.

  • Custom e‑commerce website development
  • Mobile‑first responsive design
  • Secure payment gateway integration
  • Product catalog management system
  • Streamlined checkout process

Business Impact

  • Online Market Entry: Successful transition to digital retail
  • Mobile Commerce: Optimized experience for mobile shoppers
  • Sales Growth: Expanded customer reach beyond physical location
  • Brand Consistency: Seamless experience across online and offline channels
"Our new online store has opened up a completely new channel for our business. Customers love the smooth mobile experience."

— Owner, SAGO
