Sales Funnel & Revenue Dashboard

Visualizing sales progression, conversion rates, and goal attainment to support data‑driven decisions.

Business Challenge

Sales information was distributed across multiple reporting sources, making it difficult to monitor funnel performance and revenue progress. Leadership needed a more consolidated view.

Consulting Crew Approach

Consulting Crew developed a dashboard that visualized sales progression from inquiry to conversion.

  • Funnel stage tracking
  • Revenue visibility
  • Conversion monitoring
  • Goal tracking
  • Monthly performance indicators

Business Impact

  • Improved understanding of sales progression
  • Supported performance monitoring
  • Simplified review of revenue metrics
  • Enabled quicker access to sales trends
“The dashboard made it easier to understand where sales activity was progressing.”
