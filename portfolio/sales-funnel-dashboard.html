Sales Funnel & Revenue Dashboard

Real‑time pipeline visibility from inquiries to closed deals with profit, conversion, and goal attainment at a glance.

Business Challenge

A B2B distributor tracked leads in silos. Revenue, profit, and goal attainment were only known at month‑end. The CEO needed a daily pulse on the sales funnel to make faster, data‑driven decisions.

Consulting Crew Approach

We created a funnel dashboard showing every stage: Inquiries → Leads → Opportunities → Sales. KPI cards display $4,915 revenue, $2,196 profit, 11.6% conversion, and goal attainment (118% monthly, 102% YTD). The 45% profit margin indicator and customer counts provide immediate context.

Business Impact

  • Pipeline leak spotted: Only 48% of leads became opportunities — triggered a qualification process overhaul.
  • Real‑time stand‑ups: Daily meetings now start with the dashboard, not gut feelings.
  • Forecast accuracy improved by ~20%.
  • Sales motivation: Public display of goal attainment boosted team energy.
“We can finally see our sales funnel in real time. The dashboard helped us fix a major leak in the lead‑to‑opportunity stage.” — Sales Director
