Sales Funnel & Revenue Dashboard

Real‑time pipeline tracking from inquiry to conversion, with revenue, profit, and goal attainment always in focus.

Business Challenge

Sales teams lacked visibility into pipeline health and conversion metrics. Manual tracking made it difficult to identify bottlenecks, forecast accurately, or measure team performance against goals.

Consulting Crew Approach

We developed a comprehensive sales dashboard that provides end‑to‑end visibility into the sales funnel.

  • Pipeline tracking from inquiry to conversion
  • Revenue and profit analytics
  • Goal attainment monitoring
  • Sales team performance metrics
  • Conversion rate analysis

Key Dashboard Features

  • Funnel Visualization: Clear view of deal stages and conversion rates
  • Revenue Forecasting: Predictive analytics based on pipeline health
  • Team Performance: Individual and team goal tracking
  • Deal Velocity: Time‑to‑close metrics
  • Win/Loss Analysis: Insights into deal outcomes
  • Interactive Filters: Drill down by region, product, or sales rep

Business Impact

  • Improved Forecasting: More accurate revenue predictions
  • Faster Decisions: Real‑time visibility into pipeline health
  • Team Performance: Clear visibility into individual contributions
  • Bottleneck Identification: Quick identification of funnel issues
