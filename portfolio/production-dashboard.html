Production Efficiency Tracker — Multi‑Factory Ops

Unified Excel dashboard replacing fragmented logsheets with live production KPIs and efficiency comparisons across 3 factories.

Business Challenge

Apex Manufacturing operated three factories with production data scattered across disconnected log sheets and monthly spreadsheets. Leadership had no single view of yearly output, factory‑wise efficiency, or month‑on‑month trends. Manual compilation took days, making every operational decision reactive.

Consulting Crew Approach

We built a dynamic Excel dashboard with slicers for fiscal year, factory, and product. KPI cards show yearly totals, factory‑wise output, and current month production. A 12‑month trend chart reveals seasonal patterns, and an efficiency gauge compares actual output against an 85% target for each factory. The unified data model merges logs from all sites and refreshes via Power Query.

Business Impact

  • Early intervention: A 4% efficiency dip in Factory‑3 was spotted in Q3 and corrected through shift rebalancing within weeks.
  • Faster reviews: Monthly operations meeting prep slashed from 3 hours to 15 minutes.
  • Healthy competition: Transparent factory‑level efficiency scores drove overall equipment effectiveness improvement.
“We can now see everything on one screen. It’s changed the way we run our weekly production meetings.” — Operations Manager, Apex Manufacturing
