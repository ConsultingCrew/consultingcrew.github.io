Production Efficiency Dashboard — Multi‑Factory Monitoring

Consolidating production data from multiple factories into a single, actionable dashboard.

Business Challenge

Production reporting across multiple factories was managed manually through separate files and spreadsheets. Management required a centralized view of production performance and efficiency.

Consulting Crew Approach

Consulting Crew created a production dashboard consolidating factory‑level performance into a single reporting environment.

  • Production tracking by factory
  • Monthly output monitoring
  • Efficiency indicators
  • Product‑level reporting
  • Performance comparison views

Business Impact

  • Reduced manual reporting effort
  • Improved visibility into production performance
  • Supported faster operational reviews
  • Enabled clearer factory comparisons
“The dashboard made it easier to compare production performance across locations.”
